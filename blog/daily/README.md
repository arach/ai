# Daily Notes

This folder is for short TIL-style posts about what changed today, what we ran,
and what we learned.

Use it when you want a lightweight companion to the larger Talkie-style essays
in the parent `blog/` directory.

## Recommended shape

- one post per day, or one post per notable milestone
- include the exact model/run ids when relevant
- keep the tone factual and concise
- end with the next thing you want to try

## Suggested template

```md
# YYYY-MM-DD: short title

## What changed

## What I ran

## What I learned

## Results

## Next
```
